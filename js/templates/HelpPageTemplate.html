Editor Theme

CodeBoard now supports some editor themes, you can choose your favourite theme in the theme dropdown.

The full supported list:

  • Blackboard
  • Eclipse
  • Elegant
  • Midnight

Fonts

CodeBoard now supports a wide selection of fonts, although some will only appear if you actually have them installed on your computer. If a font is detected then it will be listed in the font dropdown.

The full supported list:

  • Anonymous-Pro
  • Bitstream Vera Sans Mono
  • Consolas
  • Deja Vu Sans Mono
  • Droid Sans Mono (download)
  • Inconsolata
  • Menlo
  • Monaco
  • Monofur
  • Panic Sans (bundled with Coda from Panic)
  • ProFont
  • ProFontX
  • Proggy
  • Sax-Mono
  • Ubuntu-Mono

Think a really good font is missing? Let us know at @June.

Advanced CodeBoard

Sections

You can divide your paste into different sections by separating them with ## lines as shown below.




Creating just one section can effectively title your paste.



Syntax highlighting sections differently



You can specify a different language for each section by using it's short name.



## readme.txt [plain_text]
Once upon a time in a galaxy far, far away...

## fixes.diff [diff]
bug fixes go here
